Our Counseling Services

Akarma offers professional holistic counseling to individuals, couples, and children of all sections of society. Our approach integrates traditional wisdom with modern psychological understanding.

Individual Counseling

One-on-one sessions tailored to your personal growth and healing journey. Address life challenges, emotional well-being, and spiritual development.

Couples Counseling

Support for partners seeking to deepen their relationship and resolve conflicts. Build stronger communication and understanding together.

Family Counseling

Sessions for families looking to improve relationships and navigate life transitions. Help parents and children understand each other better.

Children & Youth

Specialized support for children and teenagers. Help them navigate challenges and develop healthy coping strategies.
