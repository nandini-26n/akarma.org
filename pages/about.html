About Akarma

Our Mission and Vision

Our Mission

Akarma is dedicated to nurturing the mind, body, and spirit of individuals and communities. We believe in the holistic development of human beings through knowledge, inner peace, and meaningful engagement with life's deeper questions.

Our Vision

We envision a world where people have access to integrated wellness practices that combine ancient wisdom with contemporary understanding. Through our programs in yoga, meditation, holistic counseling, and knowledge sharing, we aim to create a more balanced and compassionate society.

What We Offer

  • Publishing and educational resources on wellness and personal development
  • Guided meditation and yoga classes for all levels
  • Professional holistic counseling services
  • Retreats, seminars, and transformative events
